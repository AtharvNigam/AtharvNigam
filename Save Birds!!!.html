The first we can do is, don't spill waste around terestrial areas, as it will cause stink and pollution, and can also be harmful for street animals if they eat it.
There are many ways to dispose waste and in right ways
Segregation:- Segregation is a way in which we should keep biodegradable and non-biodegradable waste in different bins. Some other like hazardous, liquid and non-recylable waste are also kept in different bins



Segregation is very effetive and is used in places like shopping malls, cinema theatres, streets etc.

Composting or Vermicomposting:- Composting is a method where all of the biodegradable waste is put into a compost pit, in which microorganisms make the waste into soil which is fertle and nutrient for plants. Vermicomposting has the same process but instead of just keeping the waste, earthworms are added for more soil fertility
